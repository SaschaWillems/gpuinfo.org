Privacy police for gpuinfo.org

Scope

This Privacy Policy is intended to inform the users of this website in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) on the nature, scope and purpose of the collection and use of personal data by the websites www.gpuinfo.org, vulkan.gpuinfo.org, opengl.gpuinfo.org, opengles.gpuinfo.org and opencl.gpuinfo.org by their owner and operator Sascha Willems (webmaster [at] saschawillems.de).

I take your privacy very seriously and will treat your personal information confidentially and in accordance with the law.

Collected information

This website collects usage data and statistics in the form of server log files. The following data is logged:

  • Visited URL
  • Anonymized IP address with the last part getting remove (x.y.z.w is converted to x.y.z.0)
  • Time at the time of access
  • Source / reference from which you came to the page
  • Browser used
  • Operating system used

The data is collected for statistical use and is deleted after 60 days. It is solely used to improve the web site and for troubleshooting.

No other personal data is collected by the website itself.

Security

The security of your personal information is important to me, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage, is 100% secure.

While I strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, I cannot guarantee its absolute security.

To ensure a secure connection always use the https versions of the website and the sub domains.

Handling of personal information

Personal information is only collected and used if permitted by law or if you consent to the collection of data.

Personal information means all information that is used to identify your person and which can be traced back to you such as your name, address, e-mail, telephone number.

No sensitive personal data such as sex, race, ethnic origin, religious belief (or disbelief) is collected, stored, processed or transferred.

No personal data is shared, sold or rent to third parties.

Handling of contact information

If you contact me through the contact options offered, your details will only be used to process and answer your inquiry.

User rights

At your request, you will receive free information about what personal information has been stored about you. If your request does not conflict with a legal obligation, you are also entitled to correct incorrect data and to block or delete your personal data.
