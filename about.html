About GPUInfo.org

GPUInfo.org is a community-driven GPU hardware database for the open standards from Khronos. The databases aim at developers wanting to know what features are supported by a driver/GPU combination or to check how widespread certain features are.
The databases and client applications to populate it are developed and maintained by me (Sascha Willems) in my spare time.
No profit is made from this data, nor is this data used in any commercial way and no personal data is transferred, stored or passed.

All tools and databases are developed in the open source, sources can be found at GitHub.
